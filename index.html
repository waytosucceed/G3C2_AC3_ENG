Plurals and Singular Nouns


  • Plurals and singular nouns help us talk about more than one thing or just one thing.
  • Plurals are when we talk about more than one thing.
  • Singular nouns are when we talk about just one thing.
  • We can make plurals by adding "s," "es," or changing "y" to "i" and adding "es."
  • Singular nouns are words for one person, place, thing, or idea.
  • Plural nouns are words for more than one person, place, thing, or idea.
  • Knowing the difference between singular and plural nouns helps us talk clearly and accurately.
  • We also learnt that some words have irregular plurals, which means they don't follow the usual rules. We have to learn them separately.
